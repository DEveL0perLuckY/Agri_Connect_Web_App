Key Features

Explore the powerful features of our Farmer Management Application that streamline farm management tasks and enhance collaboration between administrators and farmers.

Admin Interface

  • Create and Manage Folders: Admins can create instances of folders based on regions or crops.
  • Assign Farmers to Folders: Admins can assign farmers to specific folders for efficient organization.

Farmer Interface

  • Upload Images and Data: Farmers can upload images and data related to the folders assigned to them.
  • Access Assigned Content: Farmers have access to the content assigned to them, allowing for easy management.
